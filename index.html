About T9Glarn

Created by: Tayten (Last name not here for privacy reasons)

Origin Story: I came up with this encoding method while I was in the shower the night of June 1st, 2025. Sometimes the best ideas hit when you're not even trying!

What’s in the name? T9Glarn combines the classic phone keypad system known as T9 (used for texting on old phones) with "Glarn," the name of my dev team's mascot. It’s a fun way to bring together phone tech, and my own creative universe.

This method uses the number pad digits paired with letters (A, B, C, and D) to uniquely encode each character — making it both simple and fun to decode!
